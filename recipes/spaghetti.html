Spaghetti

A timeless favorite, this classic spaghetti recipe features perfectly cooked al dente pasta tossed in a rich, savory tomato sauce made with garlic, onions, and Italian herbs.

Ingredients:

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps in Cooking

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Toss the cooked spaghetti into the sauce until well coated.
  7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan and fresh basil on top.
