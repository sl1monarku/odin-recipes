Carbonara

Creamy, comforting, and bursting with rich flavor, Spaghetti alla Carbonara is one of Italy's most iconic pasta dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 150g (5 oz) pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (optional if using fatty guanciale)
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Reserved pasta water (about Â½ cup)

Steps in Cooking

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Cook pancetta/guanciale in a pan over medium heat until crispy. Set aside.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolk, cheese, and pepper in a bowl to make the sauce.
  4. Reserve pasta water, then drain spaghetti.
  5. Add hot spaghetti to the pan with pancetta (heat off).
  6. Quickly mix in the egg-cheese mixture, stirring vigorously to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Adjust with reserved pasta water for desired creaminess. Serve immediately.
